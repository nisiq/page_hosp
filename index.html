What are we?

On 07/03/2023 at the beginning of the 5th edition of the ets hackathon. Our group was created, with the aim of helping the application under development drive b fleet, to somehow engage the workshops. Then the DRIVE B STARS was created.

What is our project?

Our project aims to enhance the participation of workshops in the Drive b fleet application. For this, gamification shows its main role, in the engagement and encouragement of the workshops

  • Earn points by completing everyday tasks in the app

  • Use the app and create a score for your workshop

  • Get rewards with your score

  • Among these rewards a trophy

Our path

To complete the work during a week, we separated it into four parts:

  1. Brainstorming
  2. Interviews
  3. Coding
  4. Making Trophy
